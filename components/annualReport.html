2021 Annual Report Executive Summary

October 12, 2022

This compact executive summary of the International Rescue Committee (IRC) Annual Report covers the basics: our mission, a map of where we work, impact stats and financial data from Fiscal Year 2021. In Fiscal Year 2021, the IRC served 31.5 million clients in countries affected by conflict. That includes providing 2.7 million people with access to clean water and treating 155K children under 5 for severe acute malnutrition. Additionally, the IRC served nearly 35K individuals in the United States with asylum and protection services. Through it all, our clients have been the focus of everything we’ve done and every decision we’ve made. For more impact stats from the year, an overview of our organizational strategy, and powerful stories about IRC clients, partners and supporters, see the full-length annual report.
